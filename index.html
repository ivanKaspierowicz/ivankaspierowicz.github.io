Hello. I'm Iván Kaspierowicz
Welcome to my portfolio.

I am a acoustic engineer student at the University of Tres De Febrero going into my final year. This portfolio consider some of the things i learned, investigations, and the experience that i gained.


  • Curriculum Vitae

Control room design

A fully design of a control room using the concept of "Ambechoic".

Gaussmeter

The design of a magnetic flux meter with the purpose to obtain the leakage inductance of audio transformers.

Sound isotropy

First approach to a characterization of the sound istropy from a RIR (Room impulse response) using statistics tools like dynamic time warping and bhattacharyya distance.

Acoustical parameter calculator

App developed in python to calculate acoustical parameters based on ISO 3382 standard from a room impulse response.

Cabinet simulator

Design of a cabinet simulator pedal to use an electric guitar directly to a common audio-loudspeaker.

Auditorium design

Design of a concert hall according to CABA building code. Includes 3D rendering, simulations and an audio preview.

Microphones measurments

Comparision and characterization of a set of microphones.

Professional recordings

Recordings and mixes for professional musicians in genres like jazz and folklore.
