Hobbies

some of my favourites activities

Music has always been part of my life. Ever since I was a kid and played guitar, sound has fascinated me. I have been a guitar player for many years, with denoted musical influences like blues, rock, and jazz fusion. But I was also on the other side as a spectator. I really enjoy listening to music and assisting live sound performances.

I also enjoy nature through sports. I practice sports like football, running, cycling, and trekking. This last one is one of my favourites because it allows you to do exercise and enjoy the landscapes at the same time. One of my life goals is to visit all the national parks in Argentina.

Another hobby, inherited from my brother, is photography. He's a graphic designer, and he taught me the basics of photography from a different perspective. I would like to keep practicing and learning about it, getting new lenses and equipment that allow me to experiment and improve my skills.

Finally, one of my favorite activities is to watch motorsports, especially Formula 1. I have been following this category since I was a kid, and it's a passion that I share with my family. So, it's not only a hobby; it's a nice moment to share.
