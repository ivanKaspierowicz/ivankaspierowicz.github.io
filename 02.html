Manager role

Manager role at EXOSOUND's engineering team.

2024-2025

My journey at EXOSOUND began as an intern in the engineering team in 2022. After six months of hard work and dedication, I was promoted to Pre-Sales Assistant and Technical Support Member. A year later (2024), I was promoted to Manager of the Engineering, Technical Support, and Service teams.
This career path allowed me to gain a comprehensive understanding of the company and the products, as well as the ability to manage and coordinate between different sectors of the structure. I learned many skills, such as communication with clients, problem solving, time management, task delegation, organization, among many others.


The main tasks of my role are:
- Construct the objectives, goals, and strategies of the engineering team.
- valuate the performance of the team and make sure that the goals are met.
- Supervise the technical support system and the service team.
- Assist all the sales members with technical information and support.
- Evaluate campaigns and strategies for the team with the directors of the company.
- Ensure that all the members of the team are comfortable and have the tools to do their job.
