Courses and certifications

Adquired knowledge in diverse disciplines.

This section outlines the trainings and certifications I have completed over the past few years. I strongly believe that learning is a never-ending journey, which is why I am always seeking to stay up to date with the latest technologies and methodologies.

A large portion of these courses come from Biamp, a global leader in the audio-video industry with a broad portfolio of products used in diverse applications. Given the wide range of their solutions, staying well-informed is essential. For this reason, I have completed several Biamp certifications, including: TesiraFORTE패 Certification, optimized control design for all the Impera family of products, the Cambridge Qt X Certification for design and deployment of sound masking systems, and the TesiraSERVER Certification for the design and deployment of larger Tesira systems.

Since Dante is the industry-standard digital audio protocol, I have also taken multiple courses and webinars, including Dante Levels 1, 2, and 3, as well as sessions on Dante Cloud-Based Audio Production and Dante Director. These courses have proven highly valuable in complex projects such as fixed installations with Dante/AES67, Dolby Atmos systems, broadcast environments using the SSL System T console, and redundant Dante networks, among others.

Finally, I have also completed three L-Acoustics courses to strengthen my skills in system design and deployment: System & Workflow, Soundvision, and Variable Curvature Line Source. These trainings have been extremely useful for designing sound systems and for understanding emerging technologies and live-sound applications.
