Courses and certifications

Adquired knowledge in diverse disciplines.

This section describes all the trainings and certifications that I have taken in the last few years. I personally believe that the learning process is a never-ending journey, and that is why I am always looking to stay updated with the latest technologies and methodologies.

A large portion of the courses are from the brand Biamp. This company is a leader in the audio-video industry, and they have a wide range of products that are used in different applications. Its portfolio is really big, so it's crucial to be well informed. Because of this, I have taken courses like the TesiraFORTÉ Certification, optimized control design for all the Impera family of products, the Cambridge Qt X Certification for design and deployment of sound masking systems, and the TesiraSERVER Certification for the design and deployment of larger Tesira systems.

Dante is the industry standard digital audio protocol, and that's the reason why I have taken courses like Dante Level 1-2-3 and multiple webinars like Dante Cloud-Based Audio Production and Dante Director. These courses helped me in big scenarios, like fixed installations with Dante/AES67, Dolby Atmos systems, broadcast scenarios using the System T console from Solid State Logic, and redundant Dante networks, among others.

Last but not least, I also took two L-Acoustics courses to improve my abilities to design and deploy audio systems from this brand. System & Workflow and Soundvision are the last two courses that I have taken and were extremely useful for designing sound systems and understanding the new technologies and applications for live sound.
